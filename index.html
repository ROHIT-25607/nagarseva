How it works

Three steps. No bureaucracy. Just action.

📸
STEP 01

Snap & Report

Take a photo of the issue, drop a pin on the map, pick a category and hit submit. Done in 30 seconds.

👥
STEP 02

Community Upvotes

Other citizens confirm and upvote the issue. The more votes, the higher priority it gets for resolution.

✅
STEP 03

Track Until Fixed

Watch your report move from Reported → In Progress → Fixed. Get notified at every status change.
